I am Marina Majdenić, Front-end developer from Zagreb (Croatia), former highschool teacher and language and literature professor.

Click here if you're in the mood for a little storytime...

Since I was a kid, I was amazed by all of magnificient things people were able to create. Of course, children are more impressed with the imaginary worlds depicted in books and movies, than the real world outside, and I was no different. I was going through books and movies, daydreaming and building whole different universes in my mind, never imagining that it be possible to experience something that far out of the ordinary in our lifetime.

But, the technology developed so rapidly, and just as I was leaving my childhood and coming into adulthood, it became so refined and good in creating new life experiences so that today it is almost indistinguishable from eality. A bit frightening, but that's why we should all be familiar with the way it works and educated in the ways of using it. That way we can make sure it's used for good causes and never misused.

My wish and goal is to be one of the people that use the technology as a way of helping people and creating better society, responsible and caring, but also as a way of making imagination into reality, creating and building new world, accessible and at help to everyone.
